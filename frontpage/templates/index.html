Welcome to CurationLab v0.1a! This project is meant to help people discover music outside of the Spotify algorithm, and make it easier to support artists directly on Bandcamp. After registering an account, you'll be able to edit your profile to provide your Bandcamp username (fan accounts), Spotify username, and your location. With these details, CurationLab can make a map of Bandcamp artists based on your location, create a Spotify playlist of your most recent Bandcamp purchases, and create a list of Bandcamp links to the artists in your current Spotify playlists.
