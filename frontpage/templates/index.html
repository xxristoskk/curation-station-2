Welcome to CurationStation! This project is meant to help connect Spotify listeners to Bandcamp artists. At this point in development, users can create/add to a Spotify playlist of their recent purchases on Bandcamp, along with creating a list of Bandcamp links to purchase music from artists on your current Spotify playlists.

In the future, users will be able to create maps of Bandcamp artists and talk with other music lovers.
